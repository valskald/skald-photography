About

Valentin Skald is an aspiring archival professional and amateur photographer based in Durham, North Carolina.

Although his work is informed by academic and professional training in history, libraries, and archives, Valentin's approach to photography is experiential and spontaneous. Combining contemplation and play, Valentin's work straddles the line between Fine Art and Street Photography with a focus on atmospheric storytelling, blending urban scenes, nature, and found objects into moody, minimalist compositions.

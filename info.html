Statement, process, and ways to connect.

Artist Statement

It's difficult to describe what draws me to a subject. The steps between "nothing" and "something" happen so quickly, all I can say for certain is that when I notice something, it's because that thing demanded to be noticed.

I focus on the intersection of the mundane and the extraordinary. I'm fascinated by the ways humans interact with natural and constructed objects. Recurring motifs in my work include low light, texture, and time — temporal spaces where nostalgia and unease coexist.

Process

Walking, exploring. Restraint in editing—contrast manipulation, subtle tone enhancement, minimal cropping.

Ethics

Street photography respects privacy and context. I avoid sensationalism while foregrounding relationships.
